Open Positions

Join Our Lab

We are always looking for curious, motivated scientists. Here is what we look for and what we offer.

🎓

Postdoctoral Fellows

We are recruiting postdocs with expertise in ubiquitination biology, cancer biochemistry, genome editing, or related areas. Strong candidates should have a recent PhD, first-author publications, and a genuine curiosity about mechanistic questions.

To apply: Email Dr. Shashi Kiran with (1) a brief description of your research interests, (2) your CV, and (3) contact details for three references.

Apply Now
🔬

PhD Students

Prospective PhD students should apply through the School of Life Sciences, University of Hyderabad. Please reach out before applying to discuss potential projects and fit.

To enquire: Email Dr. Kiran with your CV and a short description of your research interests.

Get in Touch
⭐

Undergraduate Researchers

We welcome motivated undergraduates for semester or summer research. Ideal candidates have taken coursework in biochemistry or molecular biology and can commit at least 10–12 hours per week.

To apply: Send a brief email with your CV, unofficial transcript, and a short paragraph about why you want to do research.

Apply Now
